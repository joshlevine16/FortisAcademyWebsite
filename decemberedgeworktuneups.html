What types of training will we do?

1) We will do a lot of advanced edge work circuits. These aren't very taxing on the legs but require a lot of attention by the players. They need to be comfortable failing (aka falling a lot!).

2) Skills - we'll mix in some light skills work as well like shooting and stickhandling.
