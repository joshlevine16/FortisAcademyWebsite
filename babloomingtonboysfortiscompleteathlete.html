Fortis Complete Athlete - Bantam Bloomington Small Group

Details

The Fortis Complete Athlete program has two main goals. First, every athlete will gain valuable skills that translate outside of sports. Two, we help every athlete reach their genetic potential and go as far as they possibly can and want in their sport.

When you join this program, we put all of our efforts into helping your athlete develop as much as possible - in and outside their sport. That is why we are limiting this training group to 8 athletes for all gym work. On-ice sessions will have more skaters.

Spring section: 28 gym workouts, starting mid-March and ending end of May.
Location: Totally Driven (6529 Cecilia Cir, Edina, MN 55439).
Spring Session Cost: $550 -- Optional payment schedule: $200 to register, $200 April 1st, and $150 on May 1st. **If you cannot make most of our workouts, you can pro-rate at $25 per workout. If you will miss a few dates, you can work with us to schedule make-ups.

What types of training will we do?

Plyometric/Speed Work: The core of our program's athletic goals will revolve around speed, quickness, and explosion.
Core & Upper Body Strength: Core will be our next priority, followed by upper body strength.
Stickhandling/Shooting: In home training program assignments will accompany our gym work.
