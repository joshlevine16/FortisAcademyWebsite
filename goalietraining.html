Goalie Training

Training Details

Training Options

ON-ICE Training: Coach Josh Waldron puts together a plan to help goalies on the ice hone their technical skills, angles, and footwork.

Gym Training: Gym work is very much overlooked for goalies. Our program is specifically tailored to help goalies develop the athleticism necessary to succeed on the ice. We focus on footwork, core strength, leg/hip explosiveness, hand/eye coordination, and flexibility.

What types of training will we do?

1) Hand/Eye: everything is with a tennis ball
2) Speed or Reaction: catch quick with your left, jump 180 catch quick with your right, etc. All with the idea of forcing the athlete to move faster.
3) Coordination/Balance: standing on one leg or balancing on a half ball.
4) Flexibility: yoga stretches.
