What types of training will we do?

1) Edge work - the key to speed is smooth edges and proper technique. We will work on this most days at camp.
2) Speed - after correcting form, performing skating sprints outside your comfort zone to increase speed
3) Skills - passing, shooting, and stickhandling
4) Small area games - combining everything we've learned into a fun game setting
