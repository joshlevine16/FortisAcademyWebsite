Fortis Complete Athlete - Metro West Bantam Group

Details

The Fortis Complete Athlete program has two main goals. First, every athlete will gain valuable skills that translate outside of sports. Two, we help every athlete reach their genetic potential and go as far as they possibly can and want in their sport.

When you join this program, we put all of our efforts into helping your athlete develop as much as possible - in and outside their sport.

Spring section: 14 gym workouts, 11 on ice sessions

Location: Totally Driven (6529 Cecilia Cir, Edina, MN 55439) for gym workouts and Brooklyn Park Ice Arena for our skates.

What types of training will we do in the gym?

Plyometric/Speed Work: The core of our program's athletic goals will revolve around speed, quickness, and explosion.
Core & Upper Body Strength: Core will be our next priority, followed by upper body strength.
Stickhandling/Shooting: In home training program assignments will accompany our gym work.
Flexibility/Yoga
Calisthenic Work
Agility, Quick Feet

What types of training will we do on the ice?

Edge work and skating: Edges are everything. The best players tend to be the fastest, most agile, and quickest. To do that, you need strong edges and that will be a main focus of our training.
Skills: We will mix in stickhandling and shooting work throughout our practices.
Small Area Games: To keep things fun and apply the skills we are working on throughout practice, we'll do some small area games.
