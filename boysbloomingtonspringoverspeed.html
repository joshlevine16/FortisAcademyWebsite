What types of training will we do?

1) We will start out most practices with edge work circuits designed to help skaters move faster with less energy.
2) Overspeed (no pucks): power skating drills where athletes push outside their comfort zone. Lots of falling during these sprints
3) Overspeed (with pucks and then with passing): We will introduce passing and puckhandling with our sprints as well.

The main goal of this camp is to get faster - legs feed the wolf.
