Train the Coaches

Developing Proper Skating Technique - Hockey

In this 45 minute seminar on the ice, Josh provides coaches with insights and tips on how to teach proper skating technique. He provides all coaches with several edge work circuits they can use to help keep things easy and fresh. In addition to this on-ice component, Josh provides coaches with an off-ice routine that takes less than 15 minutes that is designed to help improve stride mechanics.
