Jake Villas

Trainer. Coach. Mentor.

Jake Villas has nearly a decade of experience training and coaching youth, high school, and college athletes. Jake’s passion for athletics and leadership led him to founding the Fortis Academy with Josh in 2008. He has trained athletes in all sports, including football, hockey, cheerleading, soccer, lacrosse, basketball, and rugby.

Coach Villas grew up in Bloomington, MN and played high school football. After high school, Jake attended Bethel University where he earned a degree in Accounting and Finance, summa cum laude. When he’s not training athletes, he is helping companies grow as a Senior Consultant at Baker Tilly or reading the latest book on behavioral psychology.

The Fortis Philosophy

We are not just personal trainers. We don't simply provide expertise on sports training and nutrition. Our goal and the purpose behind all that we do is to prepare youth and high school athletes for success in and outside their sport. We want them to excel on the field and achieve their sports goals. Meanwhile, we believe that our suuccess must also be equally measured by our ability to teach our athletes leadership, teamwork, the value of academic excellence, perseverance, grit, and much more!
