The Fortis Complete Athlete Program

Details

We want humble, hard working athletes that will put in the effort and time needed to improve. Fortis athletes are dedicated to reaching their goal and have the desire to overcome adversity, the humility to address mistakes, and the perseverance to train when it isn't easy.

We design and help implement a training plan for your player's development. Playing lacrosse in the spring? Biologically behind your peers of a similar chronological age? Need more work on balance than speed? We take it all in and help guide your entire off-season training program.

We focus on the entire athlete, not just their sports performance. From nutrition seminars to polishing a college essay to finding a summer internship, our trainers go the extra mile with Fortis complete athletes. We do our very best to see them succeed personally as well as in the sports arena.

The core of our complete athlete program is private or small group training. The small group setting allows us to individualize training at an affordable price.

How do I become a Fortis complete athlete?
1) Complete the registration form below.
2) We will send you an email to get more information so we can put together the best plan for your development.
3) Commit to the plan we provide you.

Thank you for your message! We'll contact you shortly to discuss our program in further detail.
