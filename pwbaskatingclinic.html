PW/BA Skating Dynamics Clinic

Camp Details

Location: Shakopee Ice Arena and Jefferson High School Track & Fields
Cost: $195 per skater
Dates & Times:On-ice (Shakopee Ice Arena): June 17, 24 and July 1, 15, 22, 29
Off-ice (Jefferson High School Track & Fields): June 15, 22, 29 and July 13, 20, 27

How to Register

Step 1:Complete the liability form
Step 2:Send liability form and check made payable to The Fortis Academy to:
The Fortis Academy
10508 Zinran Avenue South
Bloomington, MN 55438

The main goal of this skating clinic is to improve skating technique and form as well as skating speed, quickness, and agility. Each on-ice session will focus on overspeed and edgework. Off-ice sessions will compliment this with skating form training, sprints, and plyometrics.
