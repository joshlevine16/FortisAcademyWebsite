How to Register

The Fortis Academy

What types of training will we do??

Most of the training we will do will be to teach the girls how to get into a proper skating position. We will also work on developing better agility/footwork and sprint speeds. Core and calisthenic training will be used as a supplement to the primarily leg training that we will be doing.
